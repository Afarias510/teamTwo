How many levels does Run have?

The game has 50 levels in Adventure mode, which start out easy but get to be quite hard by the end.

Here's a quick tutorial on the game:

Here is the starting page that shows you all the buttons you can click.

When you hover over the adventure, the circle becomes a cube. When you press the cube, it takes you to a level selection.

When you hover over the infinite, the circle becomes a cube. When you press the cube, it takes you to any level on infinite mode.

When you hover over the edit, the circle becomes a cube. When you press the cube, it takes you to a place that lets you create your own level.
